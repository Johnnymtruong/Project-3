Welcome to Mortality Mosaic

A Global Study of Risk Factors and Economic Indicators

This project explores the intricate relationships between mortality rates, risk factors, and economic indicators across the globe. Our team consists of Jessica, Ryan, and Johnny and we were driven to uncover patterns and correlations in worldwide health trends.

Why This Topic?

We chose this topic to understand how different economic indicators and risk factors influence mortality rates worldwide. By studying these relationships, we aim to provide insights that can aid in global health improvement strategies.
